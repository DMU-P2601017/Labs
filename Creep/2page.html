    • MARK DUPLASS

    Mark Duplass is not only known as an actor...

    ...he is also very sucessful filmmaker, writer and musician. Some people maybe know his brother, Jay Duplass, with whom they started film production company named after their surname "Duplass Brother Production". They have directed and written movies like Baghead, Cyrus and Deca-Pentathlon. Duplass was also the one who co-wrote the horror film Creep and was taking part in it as main character Josef. Just recently in 2020 he was nominated for Primetime Emmy Award for outstanding supporting actor in Drama series. Music was big part of his life too, as he was part of the indie rock band Volcano, I'm Still Excited!!, where he singed and played keyboard. He is married to Katie Aselton, who also played in The League and The Puffy Chair same as him. They have 2 daughters together, Ora (born 2007) and Molly (born 2012).

    PATRICK BRICE

    Donat Patrick Kack-Brice aka Patrick Brice...

    ...is American screenwriter, actor and film director, who over the past few years focused on directing horror movies. It wasn't only Creep, that he directed, but also continued Creep 2, The Overnight, Corporate Animals and There's Someone Inside Your House. He was born in California and also there he gained his Bachelor's Degree in Fine Arts in Film and Video. His first featured film was Creep and was produced by Blumhouse Productions. In 2019 Brice announced that he is already working on direction of another Netliflix film, slasher, There Is Someone Inside Your House, which will be inspired by the novel with the same name from Stephanie Perkins. Movie should be released in 2021.
